## §7 — Questions to Ask {Recruiter}

> Pick 3–4. Don't burn the clock. The bracketed categories help you prioritize what to ask whom.

**Process & timing**
- {What does the interview loop look like, what's the timeline?}
- {Who would I meet in the next round?}

**Role context**
- Is this a backfill, a reorg consolidation, or a newly created seat?
- {Question about the #1 problem the hiring manager is hoping this person solves in the first 6 months}
- {Question about how success is measured at 12 months}

**Comp & logistics**
- {Bonus target % on base? Variable structure?}
- {Hybrid cadence? Days/week on-site?}

**Save for the hiring manager (don't ask recruiter):**
- Tooling stack inside the team
- Where current programs are working vs. stuck
- How this team partners with adjacent functions

## §8 — Logistics Checklist

> The mechanical things. Do these before the call.

- [ ] Confirm {recruiter}'s calendar link and book ASAP — recruiter momentum matters
- [ ] LinkedIn: skim {recruiter}'s profile — note tenure and recent posts (warm openers without being weird)
- [ ] LinkedIn: identify the likely hiring manager — don't reach out, just know the name
- [ ] Glance at the company's most recent earnings or news — one fact you can drop if natural
- [ ] CV: confirm the version you'd send matches the personalization plan in the report
- [ ] Quiet room, headset, water, notebook
- [ ] Have the 60-sec pitch on a sticky note out of frame
- [ ] §0 cheat strip in front of you
