## §1 — Mental Model: What This Round Is

> Hiring manager rounds are about substance, philosophy, judgment, and risk. Different from the screen.

The HM is evaluating:
1. **Substance** — can you actually do the work at this altitude, not just describe doing it?
2. **Judgment** — how do you think about tradeoffs, hard calls, things that didn't work?
3. **Team fit** — would they hire you onto their team, with their other reports, with their executive sponsor watching?
4. **Risk** — what's the failure mode if they hire you? What would the first 90 days look like?

**Your job:** {one sentence — land the hero story credibly, demonstrate judgment on at least 2 hard tradeoffs, leave them able to defend hiring you to the panel and executive sponsor.}

**Do NOT:** {2–3 things — e.g., recite the JD back, ask logistics questions a recruiter should have already answered, bring up comp.}

## §7 — Tradeoff Probes & Tough Questions

> HMs ask judgment questions, not red-flag questions. Different from the screen. Examples:

**"Tell me about a time you HAD to ship something you weren't happy with."**
> "{Answer — show you can navigate the constraint without complaining, AND that you have a real bar for quality.}"

**"How do you decide what NOT to do?"**
> "{Framework + story handle. The framework alone is too theoretical; the story alone is too anecdotal. Pair them.}"

**"What's an opinion you hold about {function} that most people disagree with?"**
> "{Have ONE ready. Make it specific and defensible, not contrarian for the sake of it.}"

**"If you joined and 60 days in realized {worst-case scenario}, what would you do?"**
> "{Show you'd surface it early, frame it as a learning, propose an alt path. Not 'I'd quit' and not 'I'd grind harder.'}"

**First 90 days framework (if asked):**
- Days 0–30: {Listen + diagnose. Specifically: name 3 things you'd want to understand before making any decisions.}
- Days 30–60: {Shape priorities with team. Specifically: what artifacts would you ship?}
- Days 60–90: {Execute on the top 1–2 priorities. What does the first measurable win look like?}

## §8 — Questions to Ask the {HM Title}

> Deeper than the screen's questions. Tied to function, team, scope. Pick 4–5.

**About the seat:**
- What does success look like at the 12-month mark for this role — what would you say to your boss to defend the hire?
- What's the #1 problem you're hoping this person solves in the first 6 months, and what's the second one?
- What's something the prior person in this seat got wrong that you'd want me to avoid?

**About the team:**
- How is the team structured today, and where do you see the structure shifting in the next year?
- Who would my closest peers be, and what's the dynamic with {adjacent function}?
- What does a good week look like for your direct reports?

**About the function / strategy:**
- Where are the current {programs / pipelines / systems} working, and where are they stuck?
- What's the {leadership team / executive sponsor} most excited about — and most worried about — in this function?
- What's a recent decision you made in this function that you'd make differently with hindsight?

**Save for the final loop (don't ask in HM round):**
- Comp specifics — base/bonus/equity/grant refresh — those belong with the recruiter, not this round
- Reference questions about specific team members — wait for the offer stage
